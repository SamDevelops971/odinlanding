This website is one that lets you view desserts, drink or food, from all around the world! Tiramisu, iced coffee, or even a simple brownie. All the sweets you could ever dream of right here!

Sweets and Drinks!

Tiramisu is an Italy-originating dessert that is made up of espresso, ladyfingers, and cocoa powder.

Iced coffee is a staple breakfast drink. There are so many things you can do with it. Caramel, french vanilla, and even smores. When made at home, the combinations are endless!

Chocolate chip cookies are classic dessert that holds the title favorite in many peoples' hearts. They become even better when made with brown butter!

Matcha is an earthy green drink that originates in China. It is made out of grounded tea leaves, or matcha powder, and just like coffee, can be changed to your liking! Cold foam, honey, oatmilk and many more things to choose from!
